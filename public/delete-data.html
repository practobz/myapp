Data Deletion Request

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to have your data deleted from our systems, please follow the process outlined below.

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your data is deleted, you will lose access to your account and all associated information. Please ensure you have downloaded any important data before proceeding.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove the following information from our systems:

  • Personal profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Usage history and interaction data
  • Preferences and settings
  • Any content or files you've uploaded
  • Communication history with our support team

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • Transaction records (for financial compliance)
  • Information required for legal obligations
  • Anonymized analytics data that cannot identify you
  • Data necessary for security and fraud prevention

How to Request Data Deletion

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Send an email to our support team with your deletion request
  2. Verify your identity by providing the information we request
  3. Confirm your request when we send you a confirmation email
  4. Wait for processing - deletion typically takes 5-10 business days
  5. Receive confirmation once your data has been deleted

Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 2 business days
  • Identity Verification: 1-3 business days
  • Data Deletion: 5-10 business days
  • Confirmation: Within 1 business day of completion

Verification Process

For security purposes, we need to verify your identity before processing any deletion request. This may involve:

  • Confirming account details
  • Answering security questions
  • Providing additional identification if necessary

Alternative Options

If you're not ready for complete data deletion, consider these alternatives:

  • Account Deactivation: Temporarily disable your account
  • Data Export: Download your data before deletion
  • Selective Deletion: Remove specific types of data only
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust what data we collect

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your personal data (Right to Erasure)
  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

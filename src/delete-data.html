Data Deletion Request

⚠️ Important Information

Please read carefully before proceeding:

Deleting your data will permanently remove all your information from our systems. This action cannot be undone. You will lose access to all your data, settings, preferences, and will need to start fresh if you decide to use our applications again in the future.

How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Disconnect from Meta Platforms

For Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy → Settings
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites" in the left menu
  3. Find our application in the "Active" list
  4. Click "Remove" to disconnect the app
  5. Confirm the removal when prompted

For Instagram:

  1. Go to your Instagram Settings
  2. Select "Privacy and Security"
  3. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  4. Find our application and click "Remove"

Note: This will immediately revoke our access to your Meta platform data and trigger automatic deletion of your information from our systems.

Method 2: Email Data Deletion Request

Send an email to us with the following information:

  • To: prachi@aureumsolutions.in
  • Subject: Data Deletion Request - [Your Name]
  • Include in email:
    • Your full name as it appears on your Meta account
    • The email address associated with your Meta account
    • Name of the specific application(s) you want data deleted from
    • Clear statement: "I request complete deletion of all my personal data"
    • Confirmation that you understand this action cannot be undone

Method 3: In-App Data Deletion (if available)

Some of our applications may include a built-in data deletion feature:

  1. Open the application
  2. Go to Settings or Account Settings
  3. Look for "Delete Account" or "Delete My Data" option
  4. Follow the confirmation steps
  5. You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Profile Information: Name, email address, profile picture, user ID
  • Application Data: All content you created or shared within our applications
  • Usage Data: Analytics and interaction data associated with your account
  • Preferences: All settings, configurations, and preferences
  • Communication Records: Support emails and correspondence (except as required by law)
  • Meta Platform Data: Any information obtained through Meta APIs

Processing Timeline

  • Immediate (Method 1): When you disconnect via Meta platforms
  • Within 48 hours: Acknowledgment of email requests
  • Within 30 days: Complete data deletion for email requests
  • Within 7 days: In-app deletion requests

Confirmation: We will send you a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted from all our systems.

Exceptions to Data Deletion

In some cases, we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • Fraud prevention and security purposes
  • Dispute resolution (until resolved)
  • Aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify you

Such data will be retained for the minimum period required and will be securely deleted thereafter.

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, need clarification on what data will be removed, or require assistance with any of the methods above, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are committed to ensuring your privacy rights are fully respected and will guide you through the process.

After Data Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to access our applications with your previous account
  • Any shared content or collaborations will be anonymized or removed
  • You can create a new account in the future if desired
  • We cannot recover your deleted data - the deletion is permanent

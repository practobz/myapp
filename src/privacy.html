Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

This privacy policy describes how Aureum Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our applications that integrate with Meta platforms including Facebook and Instagram.

Information We Collect

When you use our applications and authenticate through Meta platforms, we may collect:

  • Profile Information: Your public profile including name, profile picture, email address, and user ID
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our applications
  • Device Information: Basic device and browser information necessary for app functionality
  • App-specific Data: Any data you create or share within our applications

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and verify your identity through Meta Login
  • To provide and personalize our services
  • To display your profile information as needed for app functionality
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you about our services when necessary
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our applications

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Meta platforms, as required for authentication and app functionality
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With service providers who assist us in operating our applications (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Disconnect our applications from your Meta accounts
  • Export your data in a portable format

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

When you delete your account or request data deletion, we will remove your personal information from our active systems within 30 days.

Deleting Your Data

You can request deletion of your data at any time. Please visit our data deletion page for detailed instructions on how to request complete data deletion.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers are conducted in accordance with applicable data protection laws and with appropriate safeguards in place.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification if changes are significant

Compliance with Meta Policies

Our applications and data practices comply with Meta's Platform Policies and Terms of Service. We regularly review our practices to ensure continued compliance with Meta's requirements.
